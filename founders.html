Join The Canvas Collective

Become a founding storyteller and help us share the vision of The Quiet Canvas with the world.

A Partnership in Storytelling

We are looking for a select group of creators, photographers, and storytellers who align with our vision of tranquility, nature, and modern luxury. As a member of The Canvas Collective, you'll be among the first to experience our retreat during an exclusive "Influencer Launch Month" before we open to the public. In exchange for your creative content, you'll receive a complimentary stay and a place in our history as a founding partner.

The Rising Star

(25k - 100k Followers)

Tap into a highly engaged community and grow with us.

The Offer:

  • Complimentary 2-night stay during launch month.
  • Official "Pioneer" Founder status.

The Deliverables:

  • 1 In-feed post (Carousel or Reel).
  • Minimum 5 story frames with links.

The Storyteller

(100k - 500k Followers)

For established voices who create high-quality, authentic content.

The Offer:

  • Complimentary 3-night stay during launch month.
  • Official "Visionary" Founder status.
  • Featured on our social channels & website.

The Deliverables:

  • 1 Instagram Reel.
  • 1 Carousel post.
  • Minimum 10 story frames with links.

The Luminary

(500k+ Followers)

For top-tier creators with significant reach and cultural impact.

The Offer:

  • Complimentary 3-night stay (+1 guest) during launch month.
  • Official "Legacy" Founder status (inc. lifetime discount).
  • Option for property naming rights.

The Deliverables:

  • 1 dedicated Instagram Reel.
  • 1 dedicated Carousel post.
  • 15+ story frames with links.
